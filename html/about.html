Hi, I’m Oriana.

I'm a software engineer strengthening my technical foundation through the lens of cybersecurity — because every time I look at a system, my first instinct is to ask: "But what if someone tried to break this?"

My background in software gives me the curiosity to explore how things work, and cybersecurity gives me the drive to understand how to protect them. I'm especially drawn to the fast‑paced world of SOCs and digital defense, where every log tells a story and every alert is a puzzle.

I built this blog to share what I'm learning, what I'm experimenting with, and the insights I pick up along the way. Because cybersecurity thrives on curiosity, collaboration, and open learning — sharing is caring. ✨
